RULES:

  • Always move your checker piece diagonally forward
  • Move your checker one space diagonally, to an open adjacent sqaure; or jump one or more checkers diagonally to an open square adjacent to the checker you jumped
  • When you jump over an opponent's checker, you capture it and remove it from the board
  • If all sqaure adjacent to you checker are occupied, your checker is blocked and cannot move
  • As soon as one of your checkers reaches the first row of the opponent's side, it becomes a King. This piece can now move diagonally forward or backward
  • First player to capture all the opponent's checkers wins the game!
